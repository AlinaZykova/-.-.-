The robot "Fedor" will speak foreign languages

The family of robots "Fedor" in the future may speak in English and Chinese, said Evgeny Dudorov, executive director of the NGO "Android Technology". “We (the system of speech interaction. - Ed.) Will further develop it, recruit more all kinds of word forms, in the future we plan to switch to other languages ​​- to English and, most likely, Chinese”, - quotes Dudorov TASS. According to him, the issue is still under discussion. In this case, you first need to improve the Russian language of the robot, and only then deal with extensions.

Dudorov added that with a well-developed algorithm, Fedor will be able to speak 10-15 languages ​​simultaneously. On October 29 it became known that Fedor could not use the Google search engine due to the fact that it did not pass the test for robots.

Annual monitoring of global digitalization trends

Rostelecom, the country's largest provider of digital services and solutions, presents annual monitoring of global digital trends. This fundamental research identifies, ranks and categorizes the most advanced digital technologies in the dynamics of their development and interconnection.In 2020, to prepare for monitoring, Rostelecom using artificial intelligence conducted a quantitative and qualitative analysis of more than 18 million data sources, which allow tracking the trend of technological development at different stages - inception (4.8 million scientific publications), practical significance (2.4 million patents), commercial maturity (205 thousand transactions),interest of employers and specialists (2.0 million vacancies and 7.0 million resumes), industrial applications (1.0 million publications in the media, 0.9 million search queries).
